Roadmap

March 15th, 2022
- PFP Launch and Mint -

To play and win at MUGMEN Bingo a PFP must be purchased for 5 SOL. Each PFP gets you a whitelist and comes with 5 Bingo cards. Each card will have its own unique features that are one of a kind. The card’s aesthetics possess an eccentric, yet vintage appeal that will last many lifetimes. There will be 75 PFP NFTS in total, which further adds to the worth of the MUGMEN NFTs through their scarcity and it being a limited edition.

June 25, 2022
- Bingo Game Launch and Mint -

Once the limited edition MUGMEN NFTs are all sold, then the launch of the Bingo Game can take place. The Bingo cards will go for 3 SOL per Bingo card. These cards will capture the spirit and mood of the MUGMEN appeal and timelessness. There will be 10,000 Bingo card NFTs available on launch.
