FAQ

Do I need a Mug Men PFP to play the Bingo game?

No, the Mug Men PFPs are for early fans of the project who are pre-ordering 5 bingo cards. Whichever Solana wallet is holding the PFPs will receive an airdrop of 5 Bingo Cards for Mug Men Bingo.

What benefits come with holding a Mug Men Bingo PFP NFT?

Each PFP holder gets a unique piece of art and an airdrop of 5 bingo cards when the game launches. There are even rare and unique PFPs that are reserved for the Dev team that will also result in bingo card airdrops.

How can I use my card to play bingo?

When the Bingo game launches, you will sign in using your wallet. The game will read the NFT bingo cards in your wallet and you will receive the prize money if your Bingo Card has bingo!

How will the bingo game work?

70% of the money received by the sale of the bingo cards will be locked in a smart contract. 2 Sol per day will be released and 2 games per day will happen. This will go on for approximately 25 years.

What is the reward for winning a bingo game?

If your bingo card is the winning card then you will receive 1 SOL. If two bingo cards win then the 1 SOL will be split between both winners.

How many bingo games will be hosted?

Approximately 20,000 bingo games will take place across 25 years. 2 games per day.

Why is there not a lot of paid marketing for Mug Men Bingo?

We are a small and organic community of Cryptocurrency and NFT enthusiasts. We built the project with less than 10 thousand dollars and we are funding the game with the sale of the PFPs. We are different than other NFT projects. We put most of our money into art.
